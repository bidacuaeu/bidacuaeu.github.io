COVID-19 Pandemic Simulator For Decision Making

Researchers from the Big Data Analytics Center (BIDAC) developed a Pandemic Simulator to provide a better understanding of the effects of social distancing and isolation on the development of the pandemic. To model the virus behavior, a settings panel is provided to explore the spreading characteristics in small, medium or large cities. The Simulator is user-friendly and intuitive for technical and non-technical users. It has four settings for the simulation of social distancing that would allow/disallow: to work, visit other houses, visit social areas or go to school.
